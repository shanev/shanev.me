Music Apps

I quit my consulting gig in March 2008, on the day Apple announced an official SDK for the iPhone. I knew it was going to change the world and wanted to develop for it. Drum Kit was released 7 days after the App Store went live, and has had over 10 million downloads since. It peaked at #11 overall in the U.S, and continued to be a top 10 music app for 3 years, and top 25 music app for 5 years.
